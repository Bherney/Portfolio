Meet Our Staff

Our department is home to a diverse and experienced team of professionals dedicated to providing quality education and research opportunities. Our staff includes professors, lecturers, and research assistants, each bringing unique expertise to our academic community.

Faculty Members:

  • Dr. John Taiwo - Professor of Geography, specializing in Urban Planning and Development.
  • Dr. Areola - Senior Lecturer in Remote Sensing and Geospatial Analysis.
  • Dr. Deborah White - Associate Professor of Environmental Geography with a focus on Climate Change.
  • Dr. Olaniran - Lecturer in Geographic Information Systems and Spatial Analysis.

Research Staff:

  • Ms. Dorcas Eniola - Research Assistant specializing in Cartography and Visualization.
  • Mr. Olalere Judah - Research Technician with expertise in Field Data Collection.

Our faculty members are committed to fostering a collaborative environment that encourages student engagement and academic excellence. They are also involved in various research projects, community service, and outreach programs.
