Department of Geography

Welcome to the Department of Geography

The Department of Geography at the University of Ibadan is a hub of academic excellence, renowned for its research and teaching in geographical sciences. With specializations in fields such as Geo-Information Science, Environmental Management, Urban Planning, and more, we equip students with the tools to understand and solve real-world spatial problems.

Mission

To provide a robust academic foundation and research-driven training that enhances students' ability to address the challenges of geography, particularly in the African context.

Programs

  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Geography
  • Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Geo-Information Science
  • Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Geography
